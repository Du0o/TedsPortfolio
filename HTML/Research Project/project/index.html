Data Privacy, Security, and Ethical Computing


Risks to Privacy from Collecting and Storing Data on a Computer?

Your Computer collects info about you all the time through cookies or ip addresses etc. Either way your computer tracks you and your online activity, everything that you do in the internet is tracted it is very hard to do anything without leaving a digital trail. Here are some ways you are being tracted.

1. Everytime you enter a new site cookies are collected that keep info about what you did on that site so the next time you come back it remembers what you have done.

2. Hackers may steal personal information if the system is not secure.

3. Companies may also share or sell data without permission.

Misuse of Computing Resources and How They Can Be Protected?

1. Misuse of computers happens when people use them to steal data, spread viruses, or do harmful things.

2. Computers can be protected with strong passwords, antivirus software, and firewalls.

3. Regular monitoring and updates help keep computers safe from misuse.

Unauthorized Access to Computing Resources?

1. Installing antivirus software and firewalls can further reduce the risk of unauthorized access.

2. Educating users about safe online habits is also important to prevent unauthorized access.

3. Regularly updating passwords and monitoring computer activity also helps protect against unauthorized access.
